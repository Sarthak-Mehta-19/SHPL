Contribution of Poultry Industry to the World

The poultry industry has contributed to the world in several ways. It provides a vital source of affordable and high-quality protein, helping to combat malnutrition and food insecurity. The industry generates jobs, especially in rural areas, supporting livelihoods and economic development. Furthermore, poultry production often has a lower environmental footprint compared to other livestock, making it a more sustainable protein source. Additionally, the industry fosters international trade, promoting global food accessibility and economic growth while continuously innovating to enhance efficiency and reduce its environmental impact.
